i. the pivot — coin & tag

Flip the contrast inside the chip. The pill stays dark and refined (the tag). The leading medallion becomes a solid gold disc with the glyph knocked out in dark (the coin).

The problem: v1 was beautiful but whispery — all four chips were dim-on-dim with no bright landing point for the eye. Other apps have a focal mark per CTA that pulls the thumb. The coin is that mark.

What changes per chip: the medallion becomes a real gold disc with depth (radial gold gradient, hairline rim, drop shadow) and the glyph reverses to dark mauve. Every chip now reads as "tap here." The pill itself stays dark + refined, so the brand mystique survives.
